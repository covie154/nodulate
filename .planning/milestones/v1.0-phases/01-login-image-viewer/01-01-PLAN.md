<threat_model>
## STRIDE Notes

- **Spoofing:** Use Django's built-in auth and session middleware; no demo login or bypass route.
- **Information disclosure:** DICOM endpoint is login-protected and does not expose raw DICOM metadata.
- **Tampering:** Read-only image viewer in this phase; no mutation endpoints yet.
- **Denial of service:** Cache decoded PNGs to avoid repeated expensive pydicom conversion.
</threat_model>
