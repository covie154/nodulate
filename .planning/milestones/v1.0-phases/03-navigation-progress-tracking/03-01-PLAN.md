## STRIDE Notes

- **Tampering:** Navigation does not mutate annotations except through the already-authenticated save endpoint.
- **Information disclosure:** Progress reveals only aggregate annotation counts to authenticated users.
- **Denial of service:** Progress queries should use simple indexed ORM counts over local SQLite tables.
</threat_model>
